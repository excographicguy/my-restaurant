We at Mint’s Diner, a local favorite since 1947, have been serving up delicious meals with a commitment to quality that hasn’t wavered over the decades. With our slogan, "Freshest Food since 1947," we pride ourselves on using only the freshest ingredients, sourced locally whenever possible. Whether you're stopping in for a hearty breakfast, a satisfying lunch, or a classic diner-style dinner, Mint’s Diner offers a warm, nostalgic atmosphere where you can enjoy wholesome, flavorful dishes that have stood the test of time.
